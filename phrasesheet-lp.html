Yoruba phrase sheets

Below we have included some phrase sheets that can help you develop your knowledge of the Yoruba language.


The phrases below have been collated form multiple literary sources. We recommend you check them out too:


  • Mylanguages.org
  • Modern Yoruba by Kasahorow
  • Begginer's Yoruba by K. J. Fakinlede
  • Yoruba Language - The Yoruba Phrasebook and Dictionary by Abeni Adeola

Guess what, they are downloadable too. Feel free to download and share!
